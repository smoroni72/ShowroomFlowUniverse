Clip Title

The Boutique Breathes

------------------------------------------------------------

PURPOSE

This is the very first moving image of the ShowroomFlow Universe.

The audience has never seen this world before.

The goal is not to impress.

The goal is to invite.

The boutique must feel alive before any character appears.

The building is the protagonist.

Marco does not exist yet.

------------------------------------------------------------

DURATION

3 seconds

------------------------------------------------------------

CAMERA

Locked-off tripod shot.

Absolutely no camera movement.

No zoom.

No pan.

No tilt.

The audience quietly observes.

------------------------------------------------------------

LENS

35mm cinematic lens.

Natural perspective.

Very shallow depth of field.

------------------------------------------------------------

FRAMING

Street level.

Boutique centered.

Storefront completely visible.

Open entrance.

Large display windows.

Elegant composition.

------------------------------------------------------------

LIGHT

Italian spring morning.

Approximately 10:00 AM.

Warm sunlight.

Soft shadows.

Golden reflections on glass.

Natural exposure.

------------------------------------------------------------

ENVIRONMENT

Small Italian city.

Luxury independent boutique.

Not luxury-brand flagship.

Not shopping mall.

Not futuristic.

Everything must feel authentic.

------------------------------------------------------------

MICRO MOVEMENTS

A pedestrian slowly walks past.

A bicycle crosses the frame in the background.

Tree leaves move slightly in the wind.

Light reflections move naturally on the glass.

Nothing dramatic.

Everything subtle.

------------------------------------------------------------

MOOD

Quiet.

Elegant.

Confident.

Timeless.

Warm.

Authentic.

Inviting.

------------------------------------------------------------

COLOR PALETTE

Warm beige.

Natural stone.

Matte black.

Bronze details.

Warm wood.

Neutral fabrics.

------------------------------------------------------------

AUDIO

Soft city ambience.

Distant footsteps.

Birds.

Very light bicycle sound.

No music.

------------------------------------------------------------

FORBIDDEN

No dramatic camera movement.

No cinematic flares.

No excessive bloom.

No luxury exaggeration.

No empty street.

No futuristic architecture.

No commercial feeling.

No advertising style.

------------------------------------------------------------

ENDING

The clip simply ends.

Hard cut.

Audience should naturally expect someone to enter the boutique next.

------------------------------------------------------------

QUALITY

Ultra realistic.

Feature film quality.

Natural cinematic lighting.

Premium color grading.

No AI artifacts.

No text.

No logos except the boutique sign.

Everything must look like a real movie.

------------------------------------------------------------

CANON

This clip establishes the visual language of the entire ShowroomFlow Universe.

Future scenes must remain visually consistent with this atmosphere.
