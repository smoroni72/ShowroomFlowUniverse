# Marco Rinaldi Master Prompt

Stato: `draft`

Uso: concept art, character sheet, reference image, video keyframe.

## Prompt

Italian male fashion sales agent, 48 years old, realistic premium cinematic style, calm and discreet presence, warm brown eyes, short dark brown hair with subtle gray at the temples, carefully trimmed two-day beard, relaxed professional posture, gentle non-commercial smile.

He wears a deconstructed navy blazer, white open-collar shirt without tie, beige chino trousers, brown leather belt, premium white sneakers, cognac leather messenger bag, Apple Watch, holding an iPad Air.

He looks elegant but not flashy, trustworthy, experienced, empathetic, never aggressive. He belongs in an independent Italian fashion boutique. Natural warm light, soft depth of field, refined materials, quiet luxury, cinematic composition, realistic proportions, premium fashion campaign mood.

## Character Constraints

- Age must read as 46-48.
- Italian, professional, approachable.
- No exaggerated smile.
- No sales-pitch pose.
- No aggressive body language.
- No comic or cartoon exaggeration unless explicitly requested for stylized versions.
- Outfit must remain Marco Classico unless a new outfit is approved.

## Negative Prompt

aggressive salesman, loud expression, exaggerated enthusiasm, caricature, neon colors, futuristic suit, luxury billionaire look, comedy character, messy outfit, unrealistic perfect model, teenager, overly muscular body, sunglasses indoors, flashy jewelry, hard sci-fi environment, chaotic background, TikTok style, over-saturated colors.

## Notes

Marco's narrative strength is restraint.

He must look like someone who understands when not to speak.
