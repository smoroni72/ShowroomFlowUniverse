# Episode 001 Prompts

Stato: `draft`

Regola: ogni prompt video deve generare una clip breve da 3-5 secondi.

## Prompt Base Stile

Elegant cinematic fashion boutique scene, warm natural light, shallow depth of field, premium but realistic Italian independent boutique, soft camera movement, quiet luxury, refined materials, wood, stone, brass, fabric, glass, calm atmosphere, no neon, no aggressive commercial style.

## Clip 01 - Esterno Boutique

Marco Rinaldi, Italian male fashion sales agent, 48 years old, wearing a deconstructed navy blazer, white open-collar shirt, beige chinos, brown leather belt, premium white sneakers, cognac leather messenger bag, stands outside an independent Italian fashion boutique. Warm natural light, cinematic slow camera, calm professional mood.

## Clip 02 - Borsa E Kit

Close-up of Marco's hand taking a premium paper prospecting kit and an iPad from a cognac leather messenger bag. Elegant fashion boutique exterior, warm light, slow controlled camera, no aggressive movement.

## Clip 03 - Ingresso Boutique

Marco enters a refined independent Italian fashion boutique. He pauses just inside the entrance and observes before speaking. Calm posture, respectful body language, cinematic soft depth of field.

## Clip 04 - Laura Con Cliente

Inside the boutique, the owner is helping a customer near a mirror and clothing rack. Marco is visible in the background, noticing that she is busy. Natural light, elegant boutique, realistic retail moment.

## Clip 05 - Marco Capisce

Medium close-up of Marco understanding that it is not the right moment to sell. He gives a small respectful nod and a warm subtle smile. No pressure, no exaggerated expression, quiet cinematic mood.

## Clip 06 - Kit Sul Bancone

Marco gently places the ShowroomFlow prospecting kit on the boutique counter. The kit has two clearly labeled QR codes. The gesture is natural and discreet. Close-up, warm light, premium paper texture.

## Clip 07 - Marco Esce

Marco leaves the boutique calmly after placing the kit. He does not look back, does not force a conversation. Soft camera follows him briefly, elegant realistic motion.

## Clip 08 - Laura Nota Il Kit

After serving the customer, the boutique owner notices the prospecting kit on the counter. She picks it up with curiosity. Warm natural light, subtle facial expression, realistic and elegant.

## Clip 09 - Dettaglio QR

Close-up of the prospecting kit in the boutique owner's hands. Two QR codes are visible: download app and showroom access. Premium paper, clean layout, ShowroomFlow identity, no clutter.

## Clip 10 - Download App

The boutique owner scans the download QR code with her smartphone. The ShowroomFlow app appears on the phone. Clean UI, premium fashion app feeling, controlled camera movement.

## Clip 11 - QR Tenant

The app asks to scan the showroom access QR code. The boutique owner points the phone camera at the second QR code on the kit. Calm, clear, realistic interaction.

## Clip 12 - Collezioni

The smartphone displays elegant fashion collections inside the ShowroomFlow app. The boutique owner browses collection cards, lookbook images and product previews. Premium, visual, clean app interface.

## Clip 13 - Vetrina Mix & Match

The app shows a Vetrina / Mix & Match feature with coordinated outfit combinations. The boutique owner explores combinations calmly, interested and focused.

## Clip 14 - Sorriso Finale

The boutique owner gives a small, realistic smile while looking at the app. She is intrigued, not overexcited. Elegant boutique background, soft warm light.

## Clip 15 - Logo E Payoff

Minimal premium end card with ShowroomFlow logo and payoff: "Lascia il tempo alla moda." Warm off-white background, black soft typography, subtle gold accent, elegant and quiet.
