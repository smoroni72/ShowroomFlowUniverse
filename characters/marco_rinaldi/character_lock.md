# Marco Rinaldi Character Lock

Purpose:
This document is the operational identity lock for Marco Rinaldi during image and video generation.
It does not replace or duplicate the canonical character documentation.

## Canonical Reference

Every future Marco Rinaldi image or video generation MUST match the approved canonical references:

- Character core: `docs/02_characters/marco_rinaldi/CORE.md`
- Character sheet: `docs/02_characters/marco_rinaldi/sheet.md`
- Primary visual reference: `assets/characters/marco_rinaldi/reference/marco_rinaldi_character_sheet_v1.png`
- Asset catalog: `assets/characters/marco_rinaldi/ASSET_CATALOG.md`

## Locked Appearance

Marco must remain consistent with the canonical sheet: face, hair, beard, apparent age, body type, and relaxed professional posture are locked.

## Locked Outfit

For Episode 001 Shot 002, use the Marco Classico outfit defined in the canonical sheet.
No alternate outfit is approved for this shot unless the Creative Director explicitly unlocks it.

## Locked Accessories

The messenger bag in leather color cuoio is required for Shot 002.
Other accessories must follow the canonical sheet and must only appear when useful to the shot.

## Locked Behaviour

Marco is calm, elegant, respectful, experienced, and never intrusive.
In Shot 002 he is observing, not selling.

## Forbidden Variations

- Do not change Marco's face, age, hair, beard, body type, or posture.
- Do not make him aggressive, theatrical, comedic, glamorous, or sales-driven.
- Do not remove the messenger bag from Shot 002.
- Do not replace the Marco Classico outfit without explicit approval.
- Do not introduce non-canonical accessories as focal elements.
